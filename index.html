Instant Vortex Plus

My Partner and I created a landing page for the Instant Vortex Plus Airfryer. Our goal was to create a sleek, modern, and bold design for a product that a person regularly uses in their day-to-day life. We both enjoyed collaborating on this prject together and getting the opportunity to create our own icons and graphics.

The Pixie Project

For this non-profit website redesign my partner and I, both animal lovers, decided to choose a non-profit animal rescue shelter, The Pixie Project, located in Portland Oregon. We had the pleasure of meeting with the Director and getting a detailed understanding of how their animal adoption process works. We then redesigned their website to make it more interactive and user friendly.

Obscure Travel App

For this project I worked both independently as well as with a partner for the first week. I was given the opportunity to design a travel planning app for travel junkies like myself. I wanted the app to feel like a social media app where users could socialize, share, save, and plan trips together.
